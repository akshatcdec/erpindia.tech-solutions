Overview

The Photo Upload System allows you to upload photos for students and their family members. You can upload photos in two ways:

File Upload

Click to select photos from your device

Webcam Capture

Take photos directly using your camera

Key Features:
  • Automatic image compression to under 100KB
  • Support for JPG and PNG formats only
  • Four photo types: Student, Father, Mother, Guardian
  • Front and back camera support on mobile devices
  • Individual save button for each student

Method 1: File Upload

Step-by-Step Guide:

1Locate the Photo Circle

Find the student row and look for the photo upload circles under Student, Father, Mother, or Guardian columns.

Photo upload circle with camera button

2Click the Circle

Click anywhere on the gray circle (not the camera button) to open file selection dialog.

Tip: You can also drag and drop image files directly onto the circle!
3Select Your Photo

Browse and select the photo from your device. Remember:

  • Only JPG and PNG files are accepted
  • File will be automatically compressed if larger than 100KB
  • Original quality is preserved as much as possible
4Wait for Processing

You'll see a loading spinner while the photo is being processed and compressed.

Loading...

Processing indicator

5Photo Uploaded

Once uploaded, the circle will show the photo with a green border.

Photo uploaded successfully! Hover over the photo to see the compressed file size.

Method 2: Webcam Capture

Step-by-Step Guide:

1Click the Camera Button

Click the small blue camera button at the bottom-right of any photo circle.

Click the blue camera button

2Allow Camera Access

Your browser will ask for camera permission. Click "Allow" to proceed.

Important: You must allow camera access for this feature to work. If you accidentally denied access, check the troubleshooting section below.
3Camera Selection (Mobile Only)

On mobile devices, you can choose between front and back cameras:

Camera selection buttons

4Position and Capture
  • Position the person in the camera view
  • Ensure good lighting
  • Click the "Capture" button to take the photo
5Review and Save

After capturing:

  • Recapture: Take the photo again if not satisfied
  • Use Photo: Accept and use this photo
  • Cancel: Close without saving
The photo will be automatically compressed to under 100KB before saving.
